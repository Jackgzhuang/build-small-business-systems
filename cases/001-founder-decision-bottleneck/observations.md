# Observations

## Source Context

This problem was observed in a Reddit discussion with a small business owner.

The owner acknowledged that decision ownership was unclear and expressed interest in creating a decision matrix.

## Interpretation

This suggests that the organization may rely too heavily on knowledge held by the founder. Team members may not have clear authority boundaries or a shared way to decide when escalation is necessary.

This is an interpretation of the discussion, not a validated diagnosis. No formal discovery interview or operational assessment has been completed.
