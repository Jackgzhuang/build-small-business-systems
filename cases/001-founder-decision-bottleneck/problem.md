# Founder Decision Bottleneck

## Problem

The founder is involved in too many operational decisions.

Employees escalate issues that may be better handled independently, creating an organization that depends heavily on the founder's availability and knowledge.

## Symptoms

- Slow decisions
- Frequent interruptions
- Founder dependency
- Limited delegation
- Unclear decision ownership

## Why It Matters

As the business grows, this pattern can delay work, reduce team autonomy, and prevent the founder from focusing on higher-value priorities.
